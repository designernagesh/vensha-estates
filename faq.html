FAQs

  1. Is Shankarpally under Hyderabad metropolitan authority? Answer: Yes. Shankarpally Mandal falls under HMDA (Hyderabad metropolitan development authority). Infact, HMDA has one of the 4 zonal offices in Shankarpally. Please refer to this link: http://www.hmda.gov.in
  2. What are the prospects for economic growth at Shankarpally? Answer: There is a strong base of academic institutions like IIT, IBS, Indus School, etc which will ensure the long term growth of Shankarpally. In addition, the Hyderabad city is exapanding in this direction, and large multinational companies are looking to expand in this area. Closeness to multiple express highways and local metro rail connectivity makes it a strategic location. It is surrounded by reputable institutes like Indian Institute Technology, ICFAI University, Indus international School, Global international School and 40 other leading educational institutions of technology and business management. More than 200,000 employees are working within driving distance. In fact, large land parcels are not available easily anymore in this area, which indicates long term investors have taken positions here.
