Welcome to the Asian Township

A place you can call home and an investment you can count on. Developed by Vensha Estates Pvt. Ltd, Asian Township is a community of pride & progress nestled in the lap of serene surroundings with natural landscaping, offering the much needed respite from noise and pollution. It delivers high quality housing at an affordable price. Asian township is in the vicinityto several important educational and commercial establishments. It is about 40 minutes’ drive from Gachibowli IT junction & financial district and 30 minutes’ drive from Kokapet & Tellapur. Closeness to multiple express highways and local rail connectivity makes it a strategic location. It is surrounded by reputable institutes like Indian Institute Technology, ICFAI University, Indus international School, Global international School and 40 other leading educational institutions of technology and management. More than 200,000 employees work within driving distance.
